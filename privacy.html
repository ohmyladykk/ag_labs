We collect information you provide directly to us, such as when you create an account, subscribe to our service, or communicate with us. This may include your name, email address, and payment information.

You have the right to access, correct, or delete your personal information. If you wish to exercise these rights, please contact our support team.

お客様には、ご自身の個人情報へのアクセス、訂正、または削除を要求する権利があります。これらの権利の行使を希望される場合は、サポートチームまでご連絡ください。
